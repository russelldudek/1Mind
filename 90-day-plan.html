First 90 Days

1mind · AI Operations Lead
Earn the right to scale

Entry principle: Do not begin with a company-wide AI program. Begin with observed Sales work, two bounded opportunities, explicit human and technical ownership, and evidence strong enough to decide what deserves scale.

Days 1–30 · Read the work and establish the boundary

WorkstreamActionsEvidence / output

30-day review questions

  • Are the selected workflows attached to measurable business outcomes and accountable owners?
  • Is the current work visible enough to establish a baseline?
  • Are human authority and technical ownership explicit before building begins?
  • Have existing internal tools or experiments been evaluated before adding new ones?

Early trust signals

Users see their work represented accurately. RevOps sees process and data ownership respected. Engineering sees clear boundaries and escalation. Leaders see an opportunity portfolio rather than a collection of anecdotes. No maturity score or unsupported ROI promise is required.

90-day success frame

Visible work

A credible Sales workflow map, ranked opportunity register, and clear decision rights.

Real use

Two bounded pilots, with at least one showing retained use and workflow-specific value evidence.

Durable control

Human authority, technical ownership, failure modes, and escalation are explicit.

Reusable learning

One playbook and review cadence that make the next workflow faster and safer to qualify.

These are proposed operating outputs, not guarantees or claims about 1mind’s undisclosed baseline.
